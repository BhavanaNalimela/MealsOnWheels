Our Story

Welcome to Meals on Wheels! Our journey began with a simple love for great food and a passion for bringing people together. Founded by Bhavana,Frango and Srinithi, we set out on an adventure to share our culinary creations with the world, one stop at a time.

Our food truck isn't just a mobile kitchen; it's a dream come true. We blend flavors and cultures to create mouthwatering dishes that leave a lasting impression. Every meal we serve is made with fresh, high-quality ingredients and a dash of love.

Whether you're a foodie looking for the next big thing or simply craving a delicious bite, Meals on Wheels Food Truck has something for everyone. Join us on our journey as we travel through Toronto and beyond, bringing joy and flavor to every corner.

Thank you for being a part of our story. We can't wait to serve you!
